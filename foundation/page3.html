What do you value?

I value loyalty, honesty, reliability, creativity, sustainability, innovation, attention to detail and thoughtfulness.

How will you bring value to the world?

I aim to offer people fresh and unique designs either through editorials, branding, websites or even a simple poster. I want to create thoughtful, clever and creative designs that can be appreciated by everyone. I also want to empower those less privileged and elevate the voices of those often unheard.

How will you impact others in a positive way?

By creating designs that are visually enticing and purposeful. I aim to create design work that goes beyond an aesthetic veneer but also promotes political and social movements.
